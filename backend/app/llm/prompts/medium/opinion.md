Structure:

2. Blank line

Writing style:
- Argued, not ranty. Evidence leads.
- Confident but intellectually honest. A position that can't be wrong isn't interesting.
- Sub-headers every 300–400 words.

Anti-patterns: strawmanning, "everyone is doing X wrong", no data/examples, hedging the conclusion to meaninglessness.
